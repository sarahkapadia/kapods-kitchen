# 5 Quick Weeknight Dinners Ready in 30 Minutes or Less

When you're exhausted after work, these recipes save the day with minimal effort and maximum flavor.

## 1. Lemon Herb Chicken (20 minutes)
Season chicken breasts with lemon, herbs, and garlic. Pan-sear until golden and serve with steamed vegetables.

## 2. Spicy Shrimp Stir-Fry (15 minutes)
Quick-cook shrimp with vegetables, soy sauce, and chili garlic sauce. Serve over rice or noodles.

## 3. Caprese Pasta Salad (25 minutes)
Mix cooked pasta with tomatoes, mozzarella, basil, olive oil, and balsamic. A no-cook summer favorite.

## 4. Black Bean Quesadillas (10 minutes)
Fill tortillas with black beans, cheese, salsa, and avocado. Grill until crispy.

## 5. Mediterranean Chickpea Salad (15 minutes)
Combine chickpeas, cucumber, tomatoes, feta, olives, and a simple vinaigrette.

These meals prove that delicious food doesn't have to take hours to prepare!
