# Fluffy Blueberry Muffins

These blueberry muffins are incredibly moist and bursting with fresh blueberry flavor. Perfect for breakfast or an afternoon snack.

## Ingredients
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- ½ cup granulated sugar
- ¼ cup brown sugar, packed
- 1 tbsp baking powder
- ½ tsp baking soda
- ½ tsp salt
- 1 cup buttermilk
- ½ cup vegetable oil
- 2 large eggs
- 2 tsp vanilla extract
- 1½ cups fresh blueberries
- 2 tbsp coarse sugar (for topping)

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a muffin tin with paper liners.
2. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, granulated sugar, brown sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
3. In another bowl, whisk together buttermilk, oil, eggs, and vanilla extract.
4. Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
5. Gently fold in blueberries.
6. Divide batter evenly among muffin cups, filling about ¾ full.
7. Sprinkle coarse sugar on top of each muffin.
8. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
9. Cool in the pan for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack.

Enjoy these muffins warm or at room temperature!
