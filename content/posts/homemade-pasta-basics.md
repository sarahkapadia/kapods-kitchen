# Homemade Pasta Basics: Fresh Pasta in Under 30 Minutes

Making fresh pasta at home is easier than you think and the results are incredibly rewarding.

## Basic Pasta Dough
- 2 cups "00" flour (or all-purpose flour)
- 3 large eggs
- Â½ tsp salt
- 1 tbsp olive oil (optional)

## Instructions
1. Make a mound of flour on your work surface and create a well in the center.
2. Crack eggs into the well and add salt.
3. Using a fork, gradually incorporate the flour into the eggs.
4. Once a shaggy dough forms, knead for 8-10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
5. Wrap in plastic and rest for 30 minutes.
6. Roll out and cut into desired shapes.
7. Cook in boiling salted water for 2-3 minutes.

Fresh pasta is ready to enjoy!
