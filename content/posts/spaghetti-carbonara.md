# Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

This authentic Italian carbonara is a Roman classic that's surprisingly simple to make at home.

## Ingredients
- 400g spaghetti
- 200g pancetta or guanciale, diced
- 2 large eggs
- 100g Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
- 100g Parmesan cheese, grated
- Freshly ground black pepper
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil. Cook spaghetti according to package directions until al dente.
2. While pasta cooks, heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add pancetta and cook until crispy.
3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, Pecorino Romano, Parmesan, and plenty of black pepper.
4. Reserve 1 cup pasta cooking water, then drain the spaghetti.
5. Add hot spaghetti to the skillet with pancetta. Toss to combine.
6. Remove from heat and quickly stir in the egg mixture, adding pasta water as needed for creaminess.
7. Serve immediately with extra cheese and black pepper.

Buon appetito!
